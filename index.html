About InnoHacks

InnoHacks is an exciting hackathon event designed to bring together passionate individuals from diverse backgrounds who are eager to showcase their creativity and problem-solving skills. Our hackathon serves as a platform for collaboration, innovation, and learning, offering participants an opportunity to work on real-world challenges and develop groundbreaking solutions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?:
InnoHacks is open to individuals of all backgrounds and skill levels. Whether you are a student, professional, or enthusiast, you are welcome to join us and contribute your unique talents and perspectives.
Do I need previous experience?:
No, prior hackathon experience is not required. InnoHacks welcomes participants with diverse levels of experience, from beginners to seasoned hackers. It's a great opportunity to learn, collaborate, and challenge yourself.
Can I participate as an individual or team?:
You can participate both as an individual or as a team of up to how many people you want! We reccommend you join or create a team as it can enhance collaborations and increases the chances of creating the good stuff!
What should I bring?: You should bring clothes for 4 days, swimming clothes and equipment, laptop and its charger (EU plug), anything else you think will help you, and an open mindset!
Will food and drinks be provided?: Yes, With the help of Hack Club Bank! You can bring your own food and drinks too, just make sure it doesn't go bad on the way to InnoHacks.
